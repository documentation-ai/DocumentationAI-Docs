## Best Practices for AI-Assisted Writing

### 1. Provide Clear Context

Help the AI understand your needs:

- **Audience Definition**: Specify who will read the documentation

- **Technical Level**: Indicate the required depth and complexity

- **Purpose Statement**: Explain what users should accomplish

- **Brand Guidelines**: Share your style and tone preferences

### 2. Review and Refine

AI suggestions are starting points, not final answers:

- **Fact-Check**: Verify technical accuracy of AI-generated content

- **Brand Alignment**: Ensure content matches your voice and style

- **User Testing**: Validate that AI-enhanced content serves user needs

- **Iterative Improvement**: Use feedback to train the AI for better results

### 3. Maintain Human Oversight

Keep humans in the loop for critical decisions:

- **Editorial Review**: Have subject matter experts review AI-generated content

- **Quality Assurance**: Implement review processes for AI-assisted work

- **Ethical Considerations**: Ensure AI-generated content meets ethical standards

- **Legal Compliance**: Verify that content meets regulatory requirements
