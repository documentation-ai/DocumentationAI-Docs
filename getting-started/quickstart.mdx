# Quickstart Guide

Get your first AI-powered documentation site live in minutes. This guide will walk you through creating, customizing, and publishing your documentation.

## Prerequisites

- A GitHub account
- Basic familiarity with Markdown
- 5 minutes of your time

## Step 1: Create Your First Project

1. **Sign up** at [Documentation.AI](https://documentation.ai)
2. **Connect your GitHub** account for seamless integration
3. **Create a new project** by clicking "New Project"
4. **Choose a template** or start from scratch

<Callout kind="tip">
Choose the "AI-Enhanced Template" for the best starting experience with pre-configured AI features.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Write Your First Page

1. **Open the editor** from your project dashboard
2. **Create a new page** using the "+" button
3. **Start writing** - our AI will suggest improvements as you type
4. **Add components** using the component palette on the right

### Example: Creating a Getting Started Page

```markdown
# Welcome to My Documentation

This documentation is powered by AI to help you create better content faster.

<Callout kind="note">
This callout was suggested by our AI based on your content context.
</Callout>

## Features

- AI-powered content suggestions
- Real-time collaboration
- Beautiful components
- GitHub integration
```

## Step 3: Customize Your Site

1. **Configure settings** in the project settings panel
2. **Choose a theme** that matches your brand
3. **Set up your domain** (optional) for a professional look
4. **Add team members** to collaborate

## Step 4: Publish Your Documentation

1. **Preview your site** using the preview button
2. **Publish** when you're ready - it takes just one click
3. **Share** your documentation URL with your team

<Callout kind="success">
Congratulations! Your AI-powered documentation site is now live and ready to help your users.
</Callout>
