## Overview

Templates control the layout and style of your documentation site. Choose from pre-built templates or customize your own to match your brand identity.

## Available Templates

### Classic Template

The Classic template provides a traditional documentation layout with a clean, focused reading experience. Ideal for technical documentation and API references.

### Atlas Template

The Atlas template offers a modern, visually rich layout with enhanced navigation and content presentation. Perfect for product documentation and user guides.

## Selecting a Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Template Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **General** → **Brand & Theme** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Template">
    Select from the available templates by clicking on your preferred option.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish Changes">
    Click the **Publish documentation** button to apply your template selection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Template Configuration via Code

You can also configure templates directly in your `documentation.json` file:

```json
{
  "template": "classic"
}
```

Available template values:
- `classic` - Classic template
- `atlas` - Atlas template

<Callout type="info">
  Template changes will be reflected after publishing your documentation.
</Callout>
