## Overview

Slack, in‑product chat, and email are the primary support channels. Choose whichever is most convenient for your situation.

- Slack: recommended primary channel for quick help

- In‑product chat: best for short questions while using the dashboard

- Email or contact form: best for detailed or account‑specific issues

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  In‑product chat is available from the bottom left section of the dashboard or from the bottom right section of the home page
</Callout>
