## Overview

Documentation.AI provides several community and social channels where you can ask questions, share feedback, follow product updates, and connect with other users. Slack is the primary space for day‑to‑day conversation, with additional platforms available for announcements, discussions, and inspiration.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  
  Community channels are best for general questions, informal help, and feedback. For urgent, private, or account-specific issues, contact primary support through Slack direct messages, in-product chat, or the email/contact form linked below.

</Callout>
